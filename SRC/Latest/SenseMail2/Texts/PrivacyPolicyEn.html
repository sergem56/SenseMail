We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third party sites or services.

However, we make sure that all your information leaving your device is properly encrypted and those third party service providers have zero knowledge about the content of your messages (but they know the metadata of your message, such as the IP-address from which the message is sent, e-mail addresses of the sender and the receiver, message sent and received time). Only you and the recipient of your message know the password and have access to the information sent.
